__Dungeons:__
- Command: **!!dungeon [level]**.
- A dungeon is ideal for gaining experience.
- Each dungeon includes a series of fights.
- Bullies don't regenerate before the end of the dungeon.
- Tier Dungeons (levels 10, 20, 30, 40, 50) are boss fights and must be defeated to reach the next tier.
- During boss fights, the player can swap the fighting Bully whenever they want.
- Defeating a dungeon multiplies the experience received by Bullies during the dungeon.
