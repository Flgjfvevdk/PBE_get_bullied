__Earning change :__
- The **!!payday** command is an easy way to generate money.
- Exploring ruins allows you to recover treasures chests filled with money.
- If your bully crushes an opponent 5 levels lower, you'll collect a small amount of money (not much) instead of experience.

__Payday :__
- Command: **!!payday**.
- Generates money based on the highest Ruin you've conquered and your position in the server's Arena.

__Using currency: __
- In shops (**!!tuto_shop**, **!!shop**).
- In lootboxes (**!!tuto_shop**, **!!lootbox**).
- In consumables machines (**!!tuto_conso**).
