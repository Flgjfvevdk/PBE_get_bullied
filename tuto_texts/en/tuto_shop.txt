__Currency:__
- Earn money easily: **!!payday**
- To display your total money: **!!bank**
- To recruit bullies for free: **!!hire** or **!!hire_all**

__Shop:__
- The Shop is common to all server players.
- Check the shop with **!!shop**. The Shop resets every 10 minutes.
- Rarity probabilities:
    - Toxic: 30%
    - Monster: 40%
    - Devastator: 21%
    - Sublime: 9%

__Lootbox:__
- Buy a lootbox with **!!lootbox**.
- Level required: having defeated a dungeon of the lootbox level.
- Rarity probabilities:
    - Toxic: 35%
    - Monster: 45%
    - Devastator: 15%
    - Sublime: 5%
- Can contain Bullies with specific buffs.
